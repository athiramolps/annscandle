About Ann's Candle Shop

Our Story

Welcome to Ann's Candle Shop, where passion for candle making meets dedication to quality and sustainability. Founded in 2015 by Ann herself, our small business has grown from a kitchen table operation to a beloved local brand in Durham, UK.

Ann started making candles as a hobby, creating unique pieces for friends and family. The positive feedback and growing demand inspired her to turn her passion into a business. Today, we continue to handcraft each candle with the same care and attention to detail that Ann started with.

Our Process

We believe in creating candles that are not only beautiful but also good for you and the environment. That's why we use:

  • 100% natural soy wax from sustainable sources
  • Lead-free cotton wicks
  • Phthalate-free fragrance oils and essential oils
  • Recyclable and reusable packaging
